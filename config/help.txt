todo <command> [arguments]

	<list(l)>
		Lists all the tasks in the current scope

	<pipe(p)>
		Used to pipe(doesn't have the -l fuzz) [Ex: todo . -p | fzf]

	<add(a)> [\"task\"]
		If you remember you need to watch the greatest piece of ficton 'Attack on Titan', use this!

	<done(d)> [taskID]
		If you are done with watching the greatest piece of fiction (are you?), use this!

	<clear(c)>
		Clears all the tasks in the todo (to be used carefully, no backups are created!)

The short commands (given in brackets) must be prefixed with '-'
